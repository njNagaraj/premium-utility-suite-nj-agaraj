Mastering Mobile App Iconography

In the competitive App Store and Play Store landscapes, your icon is your most important marketing asset. It must be recognizable at small sizes and represent your brand's core value.

iOS vs. Android Design Philosophies

Apple emphasizes simplicity and uniform corner rounding (squircle), while Android (via Adaptive Icons) allow for different shapes like circles, squares, or rounded rects depending on the device theme. Our tool provides all the resolutions required to satisfy both human interface guidelines and material design standards.

DPI Scale & Resolution Management

Mobile screens use various "density-independent pixel" (DPI) ratios ranging from `mdpi` (1x) to `xxxhdpi` (4x). To ensure your icon doesn't look blurry on a high-end OLED display, we generate assets at every required scale using high-fidelity interpolation.

Privacy-First Native Exports

By generating your mobile assets locally, you ensure that proprietary design work stays entirely internal. Our Mobile App Icon Generator handles the complexity of platform-specific naming conventions and folder structures, so you can focus on building your app.
